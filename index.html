About

Human-centered engineering for smarter industrial systems.

Dr. Betsha Tizazu Abreham is an academic and researcher in industrial management and production systems engineering. His work connects human factors, product design, technology adoption, and manufacturing performance to support practical industrial transformation.

His academic background includes doctoral training in Industrial Management at the National Taiwan University of Science and Technology and postgraduate training in Industrial Engineering, Production Engineering and Management at Bahir Dar University.
